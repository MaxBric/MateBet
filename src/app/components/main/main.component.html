About US

What is this?

It's a new way to use your crypto-currency.

Is it safe? Why should I trust you?

It's 100% secure. You can read the smart contract code. This is the power of ethereum.

How much ETH can I win?

Winners will get their money back plus a chunk of the losing team's pot (minus our 8% commission) that's proportional to the amount each person contributed. The site will display your expected payout.

Can I bet multiple times? How about for both teams?

You can place your bet as you want.

When does the betting close?

The betting will close at 6:29pm EST on Sunday Feb. 4th, 1 minute before the game starts.

When will winners be paid out?

Winners will be paid out as soon as the winner is available on Wolfram Alpha.

Can our money get stuck in here?

No, if a problem happened, you have your currency back.

How is the game result communicated?

We don't decide the winner, Wolfram Alpha does. We are using Oraclize.it to communicate the game results with the smart contract, with a Wolfram Alpha query for "Super Bowl LII Winner". See the smart contract.

How are you guys making money?

It costs nothing to place a bet. We will be taking a 8% commission from the prize pool.
